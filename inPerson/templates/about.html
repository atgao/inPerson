ABOUT

Here at Princeton, with everyone’s classes and weekly schedules looking so different from each other, organizing meetings and meals with your friends and classmates becomes a hassle. inPerson was created to help remedy this problem by providing a convenient platform for users to share and view the schedules of others.

Instead of repeatedly sending out forms to find a meeting time that works best, or constantly asking someone what classes they’re in this semester, simply send a request to follow another user’s schedule, and you’ll be able to view it right alongside your own.
